Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our services and processes.

What services does Five Brothers offer?

We provide university admissions, visa assistance, scholarship guidance, work visa support for Saudi Arabia, and visit visa services for the USA, tailored for Pakistani students and professionals.

How do I apply for a study program abroad?

Fill out our Admission Form. We’ll guide you through selecting a university, preparing documents, and applying for a student visa in destinations like the UK, Italy, or North Cyprus.

Are there scholarships available for Pakistani students?

Yes, scholarships are available in destinations like China (CSC Scholarships), Turkey (Turkiye Burslari), and North Cyprus (university-specific grants). Contact us for eligibility details.

Do I need IELTS to study in the UK or USA?

Most UK and USA universities require IELTS or TOEFL. Some programs in North Cyprus and Turkey offer English-taught courses without IELTS if you pass their proficiency tests.

Can I work in Saudi Arabia through Five Brothers?

We assist with work visa applications under Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, including opportunities at KAUST and other sectors. Contact us for job-specific guidance.

How do I apply for a USA B1/B2 visit visa?

We guide you through the DS-160 form, interview preparation, and document submission for B1/B2 visas. Schedule a consultation via our Contact page.

Is studying in North Cyprus affordable for Pakistani students?

Yes, North Cyprus offers low tuition fees (starting at $2,000/year) and easy visa processes at universities like EMU and NEU, ideal for Pakistani students.

What are the benefits of studying in Italy?

Italy offers affordable tuition, English-taught programs in art and design, and a rich cultural experience at universities like Sapienza and Politecnico.

Our team is available to answer any questions you might have about studying, working, or traveling abroad.
