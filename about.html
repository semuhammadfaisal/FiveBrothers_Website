Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Global Reach

2010

Foundation

Established in Lahore by five brothers passionate about international education, starting with just 12 students in our first year.

2014

First International Partnership

Signed our first direct partnership with a UK university, expanding opportunities for Pakistani students.

2018

Regional Expansion

Opened offices in Islamabad and Karachi to serve students across Pakistan.

2023

Global Recognition

Awarded "Best Education Consultancy" by the Pakistan Education Awards, now serving students in 7 countries.

What began as a small family endeavor has blossomed into one of Pakistan's most trusted education consultancies. Our founders, having experienced the challenges of international education firsthand, built Five Brothers to provide the guidance they wished they had.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 2,500 students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. Our team of 35+ dedicated professionals across three offices continues this legacy with the same personal touch that defined our early years.

"Education is the passport to the future. Our mission is to make that passport accessible to every deserving student." - Muhammad Ali, Founder & CEO
